Welcome to Students Against Tyranny - Madison

We advocate for student rights within the Madison Local School District in Madison, Ohio. Our mission is to empower students to stand up for their rights, voice concerns, and promote fair treatment for all. We aim to resist any form of tyranny in schools, ensuring that students' voices are heard and respected.

Students have the right to an appropriate and free public education, receive academic grades based only on academic performance, and be kept informed of all rules, regulations, policies, and penalties to which they may be subjected. They are assured of all due process rights and have the right to personal safety and security while at school and school-sponsored activities. Students also have the right to involvement in school activities without being subjected to discrimination on the basis of race, national origin, marital status, or other protected categories.

Our goal is to create a supportive environment where students can express their concerns and work collaboratively to address issues affecting their education and well-being.

Schools in the Madison Local School District

Madison Comprehensive High School

Madison Comprehensive High School is dedicated to fostering academic excellence and promoting student rights. It offers a wide variety of courses, extracurricular activities, and a commitment to student advocacy. The school provides a supportive environment where students are encouraged to participate in decision-making processes and voice their concerns.

Students at Madison Comprehensive High School have access to various resources, including counseling services, academic support programs, and extracurricular clubs that promote leadership and community involvement. The school is committed to ensuring that all students feel valued and respected, and it actively works to create an inclusive environment for everyone.

Madison Middle School

Madison Middle School provides a supportive environment for younger students, focusing on developing their educational and social skills while promoting student rights and participation in school decisions. The school offers a range of programs designed to engage students in learning and encourage them to take an active role in their education.

At Madison Middle School, students are encouraged to explore their interests through various clubs and activities. The school emphasizes the importance of respect, responsibility, and integrity, and it strives to create a safe and welcoming environment where all students can thrive.
