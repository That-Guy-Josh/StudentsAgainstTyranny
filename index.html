Welcome to Students Against Tyranny - Madison

Here, we advocate for student rights within the Madison Local School District in Madison, Ohio. We aim to empower students to stand up for their rights, voice concerns, and promote fair treatment for all. Our mission is to resist any form of tyranny in schools, ensuring that students' voices are heard and respected.
